About Us

“Swachhata – Your Hygiene Expert” is an educational website which spreads awareness among people about Woman Hygiene, mainly focuses on mensural cycle. This website contains statistics which has been analysed on basis of real survey conducted online as well as offline in the city. Through survey data we can analyse some meaningful data which can be used in several aspects. This website also contains blogs related to mensural cycle, woman personal hygiene, myth buster regarding periods, disease related to periods and many more.
